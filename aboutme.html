About Me

I was born at a very young age, and my real love for reading started at five. I began to excel at reading for my age, free reading behind my parent's back whenever I could. My love for reading grew and grew, and it saddened me to see all of my high school friends on their phones instead of reading books, which compelled me to create this website. This website can help me influence parents to get their children to read, and for students to realise their love for reading, and spread the love of reading to others new to it!
