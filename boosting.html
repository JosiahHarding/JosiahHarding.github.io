Boosting Numbers

 

What can we do?

There are many things you can do to prevent the extinction of books, but number 1 is to actually buy them! If we show governments and bookstore owners that we care about books and the stories they tell, the morals hidden in the story and what we can learn from them. We can help others see what we see in books. And a tip, people’s lives are usually associated with a certain genre, for instance, if a person likes fantasy games, they’ll like fantasy books.

Book clubs are a great way to read enjoyably and connect with others while doing so. Grab some friends and go down to the local library and borrow some books. A week later regroup and have a nice chat about the books you read. See if someone else read a book you think you would like to read as well! Books are a lot more fun than most people realise!

eBooks!

 

Society is evolving, so books should evolve too! eBooks are an excellent way to read on the go! With eBooks on your smartphone, books have never been more portable! You can have as many books as you have storage on your phone and read them on the train, in the car, even secretly at work! eBooks are the new future, and we have a chance to prevent books from going extinct.

Help writers and authors who love their job stay in business, and contribute to the wonderful world of books!
